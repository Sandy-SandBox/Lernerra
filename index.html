Some frequently asked questions

01

What do Lernerra courses include?

Each Lernerra course is created, owned and managed by the instructor(s). The foundation of each Lernerra course are its lectures, which can include videos, slides, and text.

02

How do I take a course?

Lernerra courses are entirely on-demand and they can be accessed from several different devices and platforms, including a desktop, laptop, and our mobile app.

03

Is there any way to preview a course?

Yes! Learn how to preview a course, and review key information about it. This article outlines how you can search Lernerra’s ever-growing library, and find courses you’re interested in taking.

04

Where can I go for help?

If you find you have a question about a paid course while you’re taking it, you can search for answers to your question in the Q&A or ask the instructor.

05

What if I don’t like a course?

We want you to be satisfied, so all eligible courses purchased on Lernerra can be refunded within 30 days. If you are unhappy with a course, you can request a refund.

06

Is Lernerra an accredited institution?

While Lernerra is not an accredited institution, we offer skills-based courses taught by real-world experts in their field. Every approved, paid course features a certificate of completion.
